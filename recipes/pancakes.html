Pancakes

Pancakes are a staple to anyone's breakfast collection. Pancakes can be made in various shapes and sizes, and have continued to be a main item of several restaurants and diners. Read on to get one of the best pancake recipes of all time.

Ingredients and Items

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Griddle or frying pan

Directions

  1. In large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center of the bowl and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a griddle or frying pain with oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Scoop 1/4 cup of the batter onto the griddle or frying pan.
  5. Brown both sides and serve warm.
