French Toast

While not talked about as much, we have French Toast. It might not be someone's first choice, but still a solid breakfast item. Keep scrolling down for directions on how to make this tasty breakfast food.

Ingredients and Items

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more as needed
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Griddle

Directions

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a mixing bowl.
  2. Lightly butter the griddle and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3-4 minutes per side.
